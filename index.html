Amalia’s specializes in home cooked food. We have a large variety of American, Mexican, Italian, and Greek options to choose from. We serve breakfast, lunch and dinner all day.

Conveniently located in Downtown Oconomowoc

The ambience at Amalia’s is one our best-known characteristics, however the food cooked up by Chef Manuel Perez and his staff are also well-known. Our dine-in experience is one to never forget.

We are open for dine-in and takeout

We are doing what we can to keep our customers and staff safe by abiding all guidelines and mandates. We appreciate all you are doing to help support local businesses during this time.
